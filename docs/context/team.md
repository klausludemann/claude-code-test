## Founders & roles

- **Solo founder.** One person owns product, growth, and ops end to end.
  No co-founder, no team.

## Decision rights

- Founder decides everything. No approval chain, no delegation.

## Bandwidth constraints {#other-commitments}

- **~10 hours per week.** Run as a side project alongside a primary
  commitment (day job / other obligations).
- This is the hard cap on everything downstream: scope, OKR calibration, and
  channel choice must fit ~10h/wk of single-person execution. Referenced as the
  binding-constraint candidate in [bottlenecks.md](./bottlenecks.md).
